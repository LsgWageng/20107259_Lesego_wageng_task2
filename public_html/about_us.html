ABOUT US

Joe's and Son services was initially meant to be a small local mechanic business but as we noticed the pandemic impacted our neighbors financially and people ended up not being able to repair their cars.
Therefore we decided to teach drop out students and retrenched workers basic car maintenance such as checking water and oil, spark plugs, timing belts, clutches and other basic services.
Three months later everyone around Hartbeesfontein wanted to be part of the Joe's and Son services and enroll in our organization.

OUR VISION

To be the leading, most recognized, respected and trusted mechanic NGO in the country.

OUR MISSION

We aim to teach everyone of all ages to have the skill of being able to repair their own car without needing a mechanic that is going to rip them off.
This will be executed by our well trained, educated and welcoming team who will guide you step by step till you are fully comfortable with getting engine grease in your hands.
