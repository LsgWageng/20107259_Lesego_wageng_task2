OUR WORK

We offer a range of services and you can choose what you want to do and for how long.
All the classes will be divided int sections according to what is easy and basic versus what is hard and advanced. When students arrive they will be allocated separate teams of 6 and each team will have one of our technicians guide to you:

BASIC SERVICES

  • oil change and oil filter change
  • Brake replacement
  • Battery replacement
  • wheels and tire replacement
  • Spark plugs replacement

after the first months of full attendance, students will then be ready for advanced services such as:

ADVANCED SERVICES

  • Starter replacement
  • Suspension replacement
  • Timing chain replacement
  • Clutch replacement

After completion of our course, you will be able to fix any car with no problems.
